# Bronze & Onyxium Crafting

A comprehensive crafting expansion mod that introduces a complete **Bronze tier**, makes **Onyxium** gear fully craftable, adds **Coal** as a superior fuel, and **auto-ignites furnaces**!

## üî• Features

### New Resources
- **Tin Ore** ‚Äî Common ore that spawns throughout caves (Y20-100)
- **Tin Bar** ‚Äî Smelt Tin Ore in a furnace
- **Bronze Bar** ‚Äî Alloy crafted from **2 Copper Bars + 1 Tin Bar = 3 Bronze Bars**
- **Coal Ore** ‚Äî New fuel ore found throughout the world (Y20-100)
- **Coal** ‚Äî Superior fuel item, **5x more efficient than Charcoal**

### üî• Auto-Ignition
Furnaces now start automatically when fuel and items are loaded ‚Äî **no need to press "Turn On"!**

## üì¶ Installation

1. Download the mod JAR file
2. Place it in `~/Library/Application Support/Hytale/UserData/Mods/` (macOS) or equivalent on your platform
3. Launch Hytale and enjoy!
